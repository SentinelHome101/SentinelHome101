Privacy Policy

Version 1.0.0  ·  Effective May 23, 2026  ·  sentinelhome101.com

The short version: SentinelHome101 does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. All scanning runs entirely on your local machine and network. Nothing leaves your computer except via two explicitly opt-in features that are disabled by default.

What data we collect

SentinelHome101 stores the following data locally on your machine in C:\Users\[YourName]\AppData\Roaming\SentinelHome101\:

  • Scan history — dates, scores, finding counts, and device counts from previous scans
  • Device database — IP addresses, MAC addresses, manufacturer names, and hostnames of devices discovered on your network
  • Security findings — descriptions of issues found on your network and machine
  • Application settings — your preferences such as scan profile, network name, and theme
  • Ransomware canary file records — file paths and SHA-256 hashes of planted canary files

This data never leaves your machine. It is stored in a local SQLite database file that only SentinelHome101 reads and writes.

What we do not collect

  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports
  • No device identifiers
  • No scan results transmitted to any server
  • No tracking of how you use the application

External service connections

Two features optionally connect to external services. Both are disabled by default and require you to explicitly enable them in Settings.

Feature Service Data transmitted Default
Internet Speed Test Speedtest.net Network packets for speed measurement. Your IP address is implicit in the connection. Disabled
Credential Breach Check HaveIBeenPwned.com First 5 characters of a SHA-1 hash of your email address only. Your full email is never transmitted. Disabled
All other features None Local network only — no internet connection made Local only

Data retention

All locally stored data remains on your machine until you delete it. There is no automatic data expiry. To remove all data completely, delete the folder at C:\Users\[YourName]\AppData\Roaming\SentinelHome101\.

Children's privacy

SentinelHome101 is designed for home network administration and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
